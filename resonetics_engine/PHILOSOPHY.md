Instead of forcing AI to "speak the correct answer," we teach it to **"eliminate incorrect structures."**

## 2. Resonance

True understanding is not the mere transmission of information. It is the **Resonance** that occurs when two entities vibrate at the same frequency.

The Resonetics Engine measures the **topological resonance** between a Premise and a Conclusion. Even if the data takes different forms, if their **intrinsic structure (Topology)** aligns, they are logically connected.

## 3. Structure over Semantics

Flowery rhetoric (Semantics) can hide falsehoods, but Structure cannot lie.

* A deceiver may use plausible words, but the **topological structure** of their narrative is disconnected.
* We are not deceived by the similarity of words; we look at the **skeleton of logic** beneath them.

## 4. The 3-Layer Architecture

This project decomposes language into three distinct layers:

1.  **Language (Surface):** The text used by humans (Input).
2.  **Meaning (Vector):** The semantic coordinates understood by SBERT.
3.  **Structure (Topology):** The immutable topological truths analyzed by Resonetics.

We are pioneering this third layer: the realm of **Structure**.
